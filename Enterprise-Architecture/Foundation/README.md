# Introduction to Enterprise Architecture

## What is Enterprise Architecture (EA)?
Enterprise Architecture (EA) is a strategic discipline that aligns business goals with IT systems and infrastructure. It creates a blueprint for how an organizationâ€™s processes, data, applications, and technology work together to support core business functions.

## Why EA Matters
- Aligns business strategies with technology investments.
- Reduces complexity and duplication.
- Enables scalable and adaptable systems.
- Improves governance and consistency.

## Outcome of This Guide
This guide helps you:
- Understand the foundational concepts of EA.
- Be able to describe core EA domains and artifacts.
- Know where to begin applying EA practices in a team or organization.
