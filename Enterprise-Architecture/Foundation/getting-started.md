# Getting Started with Enterprise Architecture

## Step 1 — Understand Current State
Document current processes, systems, data flows, and stakeholders.

## Step 2 — Establish Principles
Write a short list of high-level business and technical principles.

## Step 3 — Create a Simple Capability Model
List core business capabilities and map supporting systems.

## Step 4 — Build a Roadmap
Outline future-state projects and initiatives with timelines.

## Step 5 — Communicate
Share diagrams and documents with business and IT teams.

## Step 6 — Iterate
EA is iterative: revise models and roadmaps as strategy evolves.
