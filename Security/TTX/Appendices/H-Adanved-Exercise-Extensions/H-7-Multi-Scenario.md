# H.7 Multi-Scenario Enterprise Cyber Crisis Simulation

## Scenario Name

# Operation Black Horizon

> *A full-scale cyber resilience simulation combining cloud compromise, ransomware, supply-chain compromise, AI-enabled deception, and executive crisis management.*

---

# 1. Exercise Overview

## Exercise Type

Enterprise Cyber Crisis Simulation

---

## Difficulty Level

Level 4 — Enterprise Simulation

---

## Scenario Category

Multi-domain cyber crisis

---

## Duration

Recommended:

4–8 hours

---

## Primary Threat Theme

A coordinated threat actor conducts a multi-stage campaign targeting:

* Identity systems.
* Cloud infrastructure.
* Business applications.
* Operational environments.
* External communications.

---

# 2. Scenario Background

The organization has undergone rapid digital transformation.

The environment includes:

* Hybrid cloud.
* SaaS platforms.
* Global operations.
* External suppliers.
* Industrial facilities.

Business dependency has increased across:

* Technology platforms.
* Third parties.
* Automated processes.

Security capabilities exist, but:

* Business ownership is fragmented.
* Cloud visibility varies.
* Recovery priorities are unclear.
* Crisis communication processes have limited testing.

---

# 3. Scenario Narrative

A threat actor begins a coordinated campaign.

The initial compromise occurs through a trusted third party.

The attacker:

1. Obtains vendor credentials.
2. Accesses cloud resources.
3. Creates persistence.
4. Moves laterally.
5. Steals sensitive data.
6. Deploys ransomware.
7. Uses AI-generated impersonation to manipulate executives.

The organization must respond while facing:

* Operational disruption.
* Customer pressure.
* Media attention.
* Regulatory questions.

---
