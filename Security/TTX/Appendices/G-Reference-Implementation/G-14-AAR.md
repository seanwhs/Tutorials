# 5. Key Observations

## Observation 01

Security teams successfully identified suspicious authentication activity.

### Evidence

SOC correlated:

* Failed authentication attempts.
* Successful login activity.
* Geographic anomaly.
* Unusual access patterns.

### Positive Outcome

Detection capability operated effectively.

---

## Observation 02

Business teams required additional time to understand cyber impact.

### Evidence

Manufacturing and Supply Chain teams requested:

* System dependency information.
* Recovery timelines.
* Operational impact assessment.

### Impact

Business decisions were delayed due to incomplete visibility.

---

## Observation 03

Leadership engagement increased after operational impact appeared.

### Evidence

Executive involvement accelerated once:

* Production impact was discussed.
* Customers requested information.
* Media attention emerged.

### Impact

Earlier executive awareness would improve response coordination.

# 7. Detailed Findings

---

# F-001 — Third-Party Access Governance Requires Improvement

## Category

Governance / Identity Security

---

## Severity

High

---

## Observation

The exercise demonstrated that vendor access was a critical attack path.

Teams required additional time to determine:

* Vendor ownership.
* Access permissions.
* Approved activities.
* Emergency contact information.

---

## Risk

Compromised third-party credentials may provide unauthorized access to critical systems.

---

## Root Cause Analysis

Contributing factors:

* Incomplete vendor access inventory.
* Inconsistent privilege reviews.
* Limited monitoring of third-party activity.

---

## Recommendation

Establish a comprehensive third-party access governance program.

Actions:

1. Create vendor access inventory.
2. Review privileged vendor accounts.
3. Implement periodic access certification.
4. Improve vendor activity monitoring.

---

## Owner

Identity and Access Management Lead

---

## Due Date

YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Validation Method

Evidence:

* Approved vendor access register.
* Completed access review.
* Monitoring reports.

---
