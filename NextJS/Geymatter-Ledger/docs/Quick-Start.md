# Greymatter Ledger — Quick-Start Card

Everything you need for your first 15 minutes. For anything beyond this, see the full User Guide.

---

## 1. Sign Up & Create Your Company

1. Go to your Greymatter Ledger URL → **Sign Up** → verify your email.
2. Click **Create Organization**, name it after your business.
3. Done — your Chart of Accounts is already set up for you (Cash, GST accounts, common expense categories, etc.).

---

## 2. Add One Customer and One Vendor

| Go to | Do this |
|---|---|
| **Customers** tab | Add name + email of someone you'll invoice |
| **Vendors** tab | Add name + email of someone who bills you |

---

## 3. Create Your First Invoice

**Invoices → New Invoice**
1. Pick your customer.
2. Add a line: description, quantity, unit price, GST rate (9% is standard).
3. Check the live total at the bottom.
4. **Create & Send Invoice.**

✅ It's now recorded in your books automatically — no extra "posting" step.

---

## 4. Record Your First Bill

**Bills → New Bill**
1. Pick your vendor.
2. Add a line: description, **which expense category it belongs to**, unit price, GST rate.
3. **Record Bill.**

---

## 5. Get Paid / Pay a Bill

Open the invoice or bill → scroll to **Record a Payment** → enter amount, date, method → submit.

Partial payments are fine — status updates automatically (Sent → Partially Paid → Paid).

---

## 6. Check Your Numbers

**Reports** tab:
- **Profit & Loss** — earned vs. spent
- **Balance Sheet** — look for the green **✅ Balanced** banner
- **AR/AP Aging** — who owes you, who you owe
- **GST F5** — quarterly GST summary (internal estimate, not a filing)

---

## 7. Import Your Bank Statement (Optional but Recommended)

1. Export a CSV from your online banking.
2. **Bank Import** → upload it.
3. For each transaction: pick a category → **Save Category** → **Post to Ledger**.

---

## The Three Rules to Remember

🔒 **Nothing gets edited once saved.** Made a mistake? An admin **voids** it — never edits it.

👤 **Members do the daily work. Admins fix mistakes.** Voiding, payroll, tax adjustments, and reconciliation completion are admin-only.

✅ **The green checkmark on the Balance Sheet is your health check.** If it ever turns red, stop and contact your admin/developer — don't try to fix it yourself.
