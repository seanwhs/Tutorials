## 2. Key Architectural Pillars

* **Layered Decomposition:** Your strict separation of Presentation, Application, Domain, Infrastructure, and Platform layers ensures **loose coupling**. This allows your team to upgrade the underlying technology (e.g., swapping a caching provider or moving to a new cloud region) without rewriting the core business logic.
* **The Governance-by-Design Model:** By embedding security, compliance, and operational observability into the architectural stack, you have removed the "security debt" that often cripples startups as they mature.
* **Extensibility through Event-Driven Design:** The reliance on asynchronous workflows (Inngest) and standard webhooks turns the platform into a "composable" unit, enabling it to integrate with any enterprise HR, CRM, or BI suite.
* **Evolutionary Path:** You have defined a clear **Maturity Model**, mapping the system’s journey from a prototype to a global, AI-enabled ecosystem. This provides a clear roadmap for future investment and development.
